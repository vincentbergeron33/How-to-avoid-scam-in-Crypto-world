How to buy crypto and avoid scam?

What is cryptocurrency?

Cryptocurrency is a non-physical currency which is exclusively digital. Instead of being issued by countries, it is issued by its algorithm using participant all around the world. This system is a solution to inflation as it cannot create more tokens then the workforce of participant working on the algorihtm. Cryptocurrency also use a system called "Blockchain" to undertake any type of transaction which solve the issue of having a middle man like a bank for exemple. The algorihtm complexity ensure protecton of all users data.

What is a blockchain?

Blockchain is used to share information or make transaction with all participant. This means that all participant can see record of the transactions behind undertaken between the participants. It also ensures that no participant can tamper or change a transaction. The transaction is present as a hashtag which is linked to the last transaction hastag. Any change of any of those hashtag will reverse to an error, making it impossible for hacker to enter a hashtag block. Transactions are stored in the blockchain meaning that the speed of transaction (smart contract) is executed automatically and no delays like we are used to have using bank is required.

What is web 3?

The main difference between web 2 and web 3 is the decentralization. The web 3 is self-governing and it will distribute, verified or gives permissions to users. The best example is a decentralized exchange like PancakeSwap where you can connet your decentralized wallet and undertake transactions, invest in new technology or stake your token for rewards. Participants that owns the platform token have vote power and votes are being undertaken to upgrade the platform with new features. Small fees from transaction are used for the developping team to add those features.

Token vs Coin

A coin is a asset to its own blockchain. Coins are used to transfer money, store of value and use to buy goods and services. Example of coins: Bitcoin, Ethereum, XRP or Cardano. On the other hand, tokens are created on an existing blockchain. They will be part of the XRP platform or Ethereum platform by example. Tokens can be created by any participants.

Type of token

Tokens are created in an existing blockchain to add an utility to the whole platform. There is no limit on how the tokens can improve the whole platform and we have found over the years really innovating technology. As of now, those tokens can be grouped in 7 different types. You can find a brief description of each of them in the secton below.

Security token

Security tokens represent owenership rights or asset value link to a blockchain. It can be compare to buying stocks or bonds and it has a goal of being an investent. It is really popular right now to buy Bitcoin for an investment but since it is not a security token, bitcoin should be bough for its utility instead of oly has an investment.

Utility token

Utility tokens are meant to provide services inside of a blockchain. It englobes most of the tokens in a blockchain. They are meant to be bought for that service but can also be kept in a wallet. In example, BNB which is a token for the Binance exchange is a utility token. BNB is used as fee for all transaction in the Binance Smart Chain.

Payment token (not security)

Payment token are meant to be used as a currency. Bitcoin, Etherum and XRP are all good exemple of payment token. By example, Ripple is using the XRP token to transfer money from a company to another. Using XRP reduce the cost for all transactions and delays. A transaction using XRP is completed in 3 secs and cost 0.00001 XRP. The token is worth 0.40$ at the moment which mean, any amount of money can be transfer in 3 secs with a fee of 0.000004$ anywhere in the world. It is an amazing way for companies to pay their employer woring from abroad.

Non-fungible token (NFT)

A Non-Fungible token is often compare to a piece of art but digital. Any artist can create there own NFTs and sell them to the market for whoever is interested in buying them. The latest NFTs sold on Binance are from Ronaldo. While they can only be a piece of art, they can also have utilities. By exemple, Stepn have sold NFTs which are shoes. When you lock the NFT on their platform, you can earn tokens by doing exercices. The value of the NFTs will then depend of the earning you can make from it. There are no limit to the utility a NFT can have.

Governance (DEFI) token

Governance token gives you vote power for decentralized platform or blockchain. The platform evolve and update following the community, more token you own, more influence you have on the developement of the platform. Normally small fees are generated to substain the technical team to update the platform.

Stablecoin

Stablecoin are meant to be peg to a certain currency. By example, if a stablecoin is peg to the US dollar, 1 token will always be worth 1$. There is a big dominance in stablecon at the moment as we are in the bottom of the bear market(Jan 2023).

Memes coin

They are inspired from a joke or an image. The 2 most famoust meme coin in the market at the moment are DogeCoin and ShibaInu which are both referring to dogs. They are known to be highly volatile and have high burning mechanism.

Privacy token

Privacy tokens use their own blockchain. Their goal is to preserve the anonymity of the individual who is transferring money. Monero is the most famous privacy coin in the market at the moment.
